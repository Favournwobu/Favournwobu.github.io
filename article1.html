MSUFCU 2026 Annual Art Competition

What is the MSUFCU art competition?

The MSUFCU art competition is an art competition held in East Lansing, Michigan
at MSUFCU bank. The competition is open to high school students located in the Ingham County area.
There are over 150+ participants.

Sunday, January 25th guest speaker Steph Joy Hogan spoke about the importance of
The youth pursuing Art, notibaly quoting I'm glad I found my way back to art. Wilx
a local news channel in Ingham County attended the 2026 exhibit to inform the public of
the competition.

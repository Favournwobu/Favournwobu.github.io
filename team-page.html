Meet Our Team

Bob Ross

Bob Ross is an inspirational American artist
and educator who educates others on how to make
art. He teaches others never to give up on art.
Bob Ross is also a television host, inspiring
many young artists

Claude Monet

Claude Monet is french impressionalist painter.
Monet often utilized oil paints to help capture
landscape paintings. Monet became famous for his
unique way of painting so simply but realistically. Monet provides a creative outlook to our team.

Leonardo da vinchi

Leonardo da Vinci is a famous Italian artist and
mathematician crowning the golden rule for humans
anatomy. Leonardo da Vinci is most famous for the
Mona Lisa, a portrait that leaves art historians
puzzled to this day about who the girl is.

Van Gogh

Van Gogh is a French painter who went mad and cut
off his own ear. Van Gogh was going through a lot of
mental health problems and to help silence it and
relieve the pain, Van Gogh opted to cut off his own
ear. Van Gogh is most famous for his Starry Night painting.

Video Tutorial

The first and most important step is selecting your video!

The second step is to press the share button under your selected video. After pressing the
Share button, press the embed option, once the code appears on your screen, copy the code.

After you have copied the code, you want to paste it into your HTML document
within the body section.

Congrats! Your video has been uploaded to your webpage, but we're not done yet. If
You want to change the size of your displayed video, you can either adjust it
through your coding within the Iframe

Or you can create a new CSS file and make the size changes there, then link
that file to your HTML document.

Lastly, it is important to note that some creators do not allow you to embed
their video into your webpage. This often occurs with music.

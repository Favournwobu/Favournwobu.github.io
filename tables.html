Let's analyze what we learned!

  • Hog bristle brushes are perfect for applying thick layers of paint to your
    paintings with rather thin transparent layers.
  • Synthetic brushes are more effective when it comes to creating a painting with
    a lot of fine details.
  • Both hog and synthetic brushes are compatible and most recommended for oil painting
    Synthetic brushes allow for a smooth finish, while hob brushes allow for a textured finish
